Windows 🪟
While windows can provide good air fluctuation and sunlight, their placement is crucial. If possible, your bed show not be placed underneath a window, as it can bring out your "positive energy".
Night Stand 🗄️
Did you know that the best practice of Feng Shui for night stands are to place them in pairs? Indeed, because night stands are place closely with the bed, the pairs symbolizes harmony and balance for the household.
Bed 🛏️

Your bed should be placed in the commanding position as it is the most important item in that room and where you will spend most of your time.

If possible, make sure your bed has a good quality headboard. A headboard represents stability and support in your life, especially in your romantic relationships.

Lighting 💡
Other than natural sunlight, lights are also crucial in your bedroom. Be sure to select lights that are more soft tone and brings comfort to you.

In Feng Shui setting, it will also be helpful if you place your lamp on your night stand as it symbolizes good luck and stable energy.

Mirrors 🪞

While mirrors are great for making a small room look bigger, mirror placement can be difficult when implementing bedroom Feng Shui as it tends to bring the "ying" (more passive) energy.

It is most important is to ensure your mirrors aren’t in line with the bed as this will likely bring more distracting elements into your sight line.

Desk & Chairs 🪑
Placing your desk against a wall symbolizes a strong supporting system, which can help the household to maintain good luck in your career or studies.
Door/ Entrance 🚪
The relationship of the door and your bedroom's commanding position is important!

The best place to position your bed is diagonally from the door, so it is in your sight, but you do not look directly out of it.
